Ormskirk Lettings are committed to stress free letting. We are a friendly, family-run, specialist private residential, commercial and student lettings company with over 25 years experience in the field.

Staffed by experienced and enthusiastic property and student advisors with excellent local knowledge and empathy, we take pride in the quality of our services or landlords and tenants alike.

Ormskirk Lettings aim to be the first choice for landlords by providing a unique, unrivalled and cost effective service to help landlords let their properties quickly and efficiently and to find tenants their new homes.

We are conveniently located on the corner of Knowsley Road in Ormskirk town centre and provide customer parking.
