Ormskirk Lettings

Landlord Services

Ormskirk Lettings aim to be the first choice for landlords by providing a unique, unrivalled and cost effective service to help landlords let their properties quickly and efficiently and to find tenants their new homes. Ormskirk Lettings will advise landlords on how to maximise rental income. Click the following for more information on our outstanding services:

We will visit your property to advise on the best use of available space in order to maximise rental income. Sometimes there are simple solutions to creating more rooms and updating existing accommodation.

To simplify the process for landlords we offer a full project management service, which includes design and Build , planning permission, decorating, furniture packs, floor covering and window dressings.

To help rent your properties quickly and efficiently, we will advertise them on our website which feeds to well known property portals, Rightmove and OnTheMarket.

We will also advise on appropriate rents and terms of tenancy as well as suitable tenant types for your properties.
